# Supply-Chain-Segmentation-Analysis-
# **Objective**
AtliQ Mart is a growing FMCG (fast moving consumer Good) manufacturer headquartered in Gujarat, India. It is currently operational in three cities Vadodara, Surat and Ahmedabad. They want to expand to other metro/tier1 cities in the next 2 years.

# **Dataset**
https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1AkVENpnXau9MdW87bVmLtuBFap8R2i6m


# **Tools**
Power Bi , Quadratic 

# **Conclusions**

•	Ahmedabad is the only City Which Contributes most of the orders.

•	The Dairy product is mostly order in all the cities its high in demand.

•	Beverages have the lowest order share

•	The Vijay store, Rel fresh, Lotus Mart, Propel Mart, Acclaimed Stores Contribute the highest percentage of order Overall 8.40% to 8.70%.

•	The demand is Concentrated only in few stores which are Contributing most of the orders stores like Vijay Store and Rel Fresh.

•	Surat performs slightly better in on-time deliveries.

•	Vadodara is the lowest performing city overall.

•	OTIF% = 48%, This means more than half the orders fail service standards.

•	IF% = 65.96%, Many orders are shipped with missing items.

•	OT% = 71.12%, Almost 30% of deliveries are late

•	Atlas Stores, Chiptec Stores, Propel Mart, Expert Mart. These customers have strong supply chain service levels.

•	Many customers cluster around 80-90% OT and 60-75% IF

•	The supply chain consistently underperforms the target by 8-10%

•	Weekly IF% Trend Lowest ≈ 63% Highest ≈ 67-68% performance is stable but consistently below target.

•	Vadodara consistently performs worst in fulfillment.

•	Food category has the lowest fulfillment rate.

•	Weekly OT% fluctuates between approximately 69% to 73%

•	The company consistently misses the delivery target by 6-9%.

•	Vadodara has the lowest On-Time delivery performance.

•	Food products have the most delivery delays.

•	Weekly LIFR fluctuates around 63% – 67%

•	Vadodara has the lowest line-item fulfillment performance.

•	Food products have the lowest fill rate.

•	Weekly VOFR fluctuates around: 96.3% – 96.9%

•	All product categories maintain very high-volume 

•	38K order lines were delivered completely

•	Vijay Stores, Rel Fresh, Propel Mart, Acclaimed Stores place the highest number of orders.

•	Customers Elite Mart (52%), Coolblue (51%), Info Stores (53%) with low LIFR% rate

•	One-third of order lines are incomplete

•	Most orders are delivered on time, but a significant portion experiences 1-day delays.

•	City-level analysis shows consistent performance across Ahmedabad, Surat, and Vadodara, indicating that delays are likely caused by operational inefficiencies

•	The biggest operational issue is delayed deliveries, not early or on-time ones.

•	Even though on-time delivery is good, the delay rate 15% average is still significant.

•	The biggest supply chain problem is not quantity fulfillment but product availability.

•	Customers are receiving most of the volume, but not the exact products they ordered.
